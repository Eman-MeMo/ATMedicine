who are we ?


We are a pharmaceutical company (ATMedicine)
offering new and unprecedented practical models of services such as:
buying medicines from our machine anywhere near you without
resorting to pharmacies The goal of atmedicine is health and personal
care in Egypt and we will continue our development
by spreading throughout the Republic to be by your side always and
at any time and in the easiest possible way

Our Vision


Our vision is to bring safe, fast and convenient access to
medicine to everyone across the world.

Our Mission


Our Mission is to be a champion of safe & convenient pharmaceutical
access in the case of emergencies, that is available at all the times in
underserved areas and travel routes.

Our Goal


  • Make our ATM network available in all major districts
    of greater Cairo width one year

  • Have four ATMs available on the following travel roads
    within two years:
    • Cairo-Sokhna
    • Cairo-Alexandria
    • Cairo-Hurghada
    • Cairo-Luxor
